Venn Diagrams

  • created by John Venn (1834-1923)
  • used to diagram statements and arguments
    • an category contains an X if the category contains an item
    • the X can also be placed outside the category circle
    • a category is shaded if the category contains nothing
    • shading outside the circle means nothing is outside the category
  • subject circle goes on left; predicate circle goes on right
